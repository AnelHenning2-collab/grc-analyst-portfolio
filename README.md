## Portfolio Contents

### 01 · Risk Management
A complete risk management workflow modeled on NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF):
- **Risk Register** with 15 sample enterprise risk scenarios across IT, operational, and third-party domains
- Qualitative and quantitative risk scoring (likelihood × impact matrix)
- Risk owner assignment, treatment options, and residual risk tracking

### 02 · Compliance Framework Mapping
Cross-framework control mapping showing alignment between major standards:
- **NIST CSF 2.0** (Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover)
- **ISO/IEC 27001:2022** (Annex A controls)
- **SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria** (Security, Availability, Confidentiality)
- Practical gap analysis methodology

### 03 · Policy Writing Samples
Three professionally written security policies demonstrating documentation skills:
- **Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)** — covers devices, internet, email, and data handling
- **Incident Response Policy (IRP)** — detection, classification, escalation, and recovery
- **Access Control Policy (ACP)** — least privilege, access reviews, and offboarding

### 04 · Audit & Controls
Internal audit readiness materials:
- **SOC 2 Type II Checklist** — 40+ controls mapped to Trust Services Criteria
- Gap analysis template showing current vs. required state
- Evidence collection guide for audit support
